> "Real analysis is the art of estimating."

The inequality we'll use everywhere is the **triangle inequality**:

$$|x + y| \le |x| + |y| \qquad \forall\, x, y \in \mathbb{R}.$$

From it, by induction, follows the version for finite sums:

$$\left| \sum_{k=1}^{n} x_k \right| \le \sum_{k=1}^{n} |x_k|.$$
